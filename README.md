qlanth is a Mathematica package for calculating the level structure, and magnetic dipole transition rates of lanthanoid ions embedded in crystals.

It was originally created by Christopher Dodson, and is currently being maintained and expanded by David Lizarazo.

## References

- Dodson, Christopher M., and Rashid Zia. “Magnetic Dipole and Electric Quadrupole Transitions in the Trivalent Lanthanide Series: Calculated Emission Rates and Oscillator Strengths.” Physical Review B 86, no. 12 (September 5, 2012): 125102. https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevB.86.125102.
